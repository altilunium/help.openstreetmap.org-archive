As mentioned, the age varies from each source. Even with the same source of imagery, the age will change (as noted on the bing page) depending on the particular place.

Right now, there isn't a specific place detailing all of this information, but you could check out: http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Aerial_imagery and http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Potential_Datasources

Also, if you're a josm user: Have the WMS layer open and selected, right-click on it on any area and select 'tile info'. This will sometimes tell you the approximate date when the imagery was taken (although this is sometimes incorrect!).
