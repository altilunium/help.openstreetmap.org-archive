OSM's maps are usually pretty accurate, however we still lack a lot of house numbers. Chances are house numbers are not, or not adequately, mapped in your area, so OSM has no idea where exactly on the street your house is, and places the marker somewhere in the middle of the street.

You can help improving the map by recording house numbers in your area.
