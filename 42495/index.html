Why does this site require third-party resources?

0

When I use this site, most things work fine, but I'm forced to load resources from google.com and ajax.googleapis.com for the following:

  • actual submission of questions (otherwise, nothing happens when hitting "Ask your question"),
  • tag and duplicate question suggestions, preview (just a blank space is shown in their place).

Why compromise user privacy by loading stuff from Google? Surely the required resources could be stored locally on this domain. Is there a configuration option for this, or would it need to be added upstream? Does OSM maintain local changes to OSQA? Where are settings and local alterations kept?

(I use NoScript to see and tailor what resources are used from where.)
