You may find tools that use OSM-based maps here:

https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Edit_GPS_tracks#Options (list "Options" at the bottom) or at https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Software/Desktop#Track_making_features .

For Android see https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Android ("Navigation" or "Track recording" setions). I, personally, have used OsmAnd (also on F-Droid) for loading, displaying and following gpx tracks.
