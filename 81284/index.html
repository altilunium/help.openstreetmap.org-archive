Hi MK0815,

welcome to OpenStreetMap and thanks for contributing!

On my side the new road is shown properly on all zoom levels. As you suspected it is probably a matter of some cache on the way to your browser still serving the old tiles. It will be solved over time. In the meantime you can try to reload the page without using the cache (possibly Ctrl+F5).

The OSRM router on openstreetmap.org is also using the new road. Graphhopper probably still needs a day or two to also pick up the changes.

No need to worry.

On a side node: At least in Germany it seems to be consensus that the lanes= key should only be set if there are explicit lane markings on the ground or lanes are very obvious from other aspects. In a residential street with maxspeed 30 this seems to be unlikely to me. You might want to consider removing the lanes= key altogether and tagging a width instead but of course you will have local knowledge I don't.
