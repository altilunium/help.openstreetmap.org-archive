Apparently some other sources cover iceland, and are used for the DEMs at http://www.viewfinderpanoramas.org/dem3.html#nat . Please check their terms and conditions before use.

I found this link by using "iceland dem" as my google search query.

I don't know if OpenCycleMap uses this DEM or some other. On their doc page at http://www.opencyclemap.org/docs/ they (that is, Andy Allan) states they use SRTM, but that may be inaccurate, as there seems to be no SRTM coverage of iceland...
