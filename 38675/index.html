I think that the mapping of private house gardens would be too much work (with the chance of rapid changes).

I have to create about 400 Houses, 800 other Buildings like car parks, sheds, barns and stables, add about 80 PoIs and 2000 Adresses/House Numbers in my living town yet. Mapping of grass is for me out of question. :-) And the towns in the neighborhood are waiting with nearly the same work to do.

Therefore I would map only public parks and maybe the surroundings of public buildings (schools, town halls, churches ...) and than maybe - pictorially spoken - look over the fence, which town in the neighborhood needs help.
