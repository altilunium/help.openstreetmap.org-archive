As far as I know, there is no automatic building import on a global level.

In some places, e.g. France, there are building imports from official sources (cadastre), but in other regions, e.g. Sweden, there are no such efforts underway.

Sooner or later, there will probably be an AI that just grabs buildings and everything else off aerial imagery, but I would not hold my breath waiting. :)

OpenStreetMap's strength is its community of human beings dedicated to improve the map, not algorithms that analyse aerial imagery. Algorithms can occasionally help the human mapper but they cannot replace him or her.
