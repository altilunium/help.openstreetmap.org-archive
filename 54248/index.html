The licence applies in perpetuity for all OSM data. If you build on and improve OSM data, you have to make those improvements available under the same licence.

I would recommend reading the full licence text at http://opendatacommons.org/licenses/odbl/1.0/ and the Community Guidelines (particularly the 'Horizontal Layers' and 'Collective Database' ones) at http://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Licence/Community_Guidelines .
