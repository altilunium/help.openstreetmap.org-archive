Residential Road - Does not go thru

1

There is a road in my town that shows up on the map as a thru road from one intersection (intersection 1) to the next intersection (intersection 2). That is not the case.

In the midpoint between intersection 1 and intersection 2 is a permanent barrier (a guardrail) that prevents a person attempting to navigate thru.

The break or barrier on this road can be seen on the satellite image, but the road layer is drawn as a thru road.

What is the best way to illustrate or change this issue on the map using the tools available?

Please note, I am new to OpenStreetMap and attempting my first correction.

Very simple: add a node to the road at the position and tag it as a barrier. To do this you can use the online editor on openstreetmap.org (you will need an account).
