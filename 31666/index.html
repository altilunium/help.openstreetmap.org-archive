This question is not really related to Potlatch2. The only issue is that Potlatch2 does not help you with special characters itself (which is really not wrong). As far as I know the other OSM editors also do not offer a help themselves with this.

  • There is the method described by Hendrik: use a "special character insert" tool/table from another program and copy & paste then.
  • You can use whatever your operating system supplies — special character tables.
  • You also could copy the character from a website like Wikipedia (article about "o").
  • But you may also just press the correct keys on your keyboard (which and if possible depends on your keyboard mapping).
