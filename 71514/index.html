The answer is simple in the end. Komoot is using somewhat outdated data. The culprit in the routing lies on a small piece of road as you can find out by moving start and end of the route around. Routing works the other way round. This stretch of road was falsely tagged as a oneway road until two months ago. Komoot apparently uses data from before the correction,
