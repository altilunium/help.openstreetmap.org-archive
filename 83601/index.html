OSM has historic data but that will only tell you when something has been added to OSM, not when something has been built. As a trivial example, looking at the 2005 archive data would tell you that there wasn't a single building on the planet.

Some mappers record information on when something has been built, usually in the "start_date" tag, but you will find this relatively rarely used. There's also http://openhistoricalmap.org/ which, unlike OSM, aims to also record things that have ceased to exist.
