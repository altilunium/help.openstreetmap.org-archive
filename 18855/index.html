In practice, all areas use whatever is most practical. In the US highway=motorway and highway=primary are the defaults for Interstates and US roads, but secondary roads are commonly used for state roads. In Canada, these are used for provincial roads

You gave to remember that the original tagging guidelines were written with the UK in mind. The us road guidelines are on the wiki here and here.
