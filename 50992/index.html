When you click the "share" button on the right hand side of the map to export an image, the scale will be populated with the value matching your current zoom level. Take note of the scale value that is suggested for the two zoom levels that you want to have an "in-between" version of (you have to close and re-open the share panel after zooming). Then run an image export with a scale number in between the two numbers suggested.
