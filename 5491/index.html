Openstreetmap isn't really a GPX sharing site; people upload GPX files here just so that the underlying paths can be added to the map.

However, if you zoom into the map in the region that you're interested in, click edit, and then show GPS traces you can see what people have uploaded. Unfortunately it looks like no-one's crossed the border there; there are GPS traces which have been mapped as paths up to the Cascada de Gavarnie on the French side and a path part of the way to Refugio de Goriz on the Spanish side.

If you do walk it please do upload your GPS traces here!
