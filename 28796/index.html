amenity=hospital is a good start.

One way to refine it further is health_specialty=psychotherapy/psychiatry. It comes from the healthcare 2.0 proposed feature, which has seen some criticism and may not yet be ready for prime-time, but seems to be the only comprehensive proposition and sees some traction.

The other option I know of is healthcare=psychotherapist / psychiatrist from the older healthcare proposed feature. It's a bit simpler and is slightly more frequent in the db.

Unless you have a strong preference for healthcare 1 or 2, I suggest you tag both, as their tags do not collide and you'll reach a wider "audience" since neither scheme has clearly "won" yet.

That said, most mental institutions are much more lightweight than a hospital. amenity=social_facility is often a better fit, and includes a fairly well-used social_facility:for=mental_health.
