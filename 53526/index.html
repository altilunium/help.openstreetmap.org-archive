Good resources to start are the beginner's wiki and learnosm.

If you want to just show your own data on top of OSM, umap is often the answer. More info. If you have your own data you want to integrate into OSM itself, a good place to start is the Import guidelines. In general, it is recomended to really get to know OSM before getting into that.
