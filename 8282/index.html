Are these your edits: http://www.openstreetmap.org/user/Bennet%20Campoverde/edits

If so the ways you have added have no tags to say that they are highways, such as http://www.openstreetmap.org/browse/way/131916513 and http://www.openstreetmap.org/browse/way/131916590
