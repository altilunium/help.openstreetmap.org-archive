You can write to the admins of umap and hope that they will help you. See "Feedback and Help" on https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/UMap.

If that does not help, you can at least partially rescue your data by accessing and saving the individual layers you have created

http://umap.openstreetmap.fr/de/datalayer/681017/ http://umap.openstreetmap.fr/de/datalayer/681016/ http://umap.openstreetmap.fr/de/datalayer/681015/ http://umap.openstreetmap.fr/de/datalayer/681013/ http://umap.openstreetmap.fr/de/datalayer/680959/ http://umap.openstreetmap.fr/de/datalayer/680946/ http://umap.openstreetmap.fr/de/datalayer/681040/

and uploading them into a new map (this time saving the edit link...)
