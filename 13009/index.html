You can also tag an area as a highway, with area=yes. Example:

highway=pedestrian

area=yes

name=XYZ

In Potlatch 2 "simple mode", click on the second register (the one, that looks like a checkbox), expand the menu "physical" and check the "open area" checkbox.

You can also add other tags as needed, e.g. amenity=parking, amenity=marketplace and so on
