You can choose the Link option 3rd from left at Top Left of screen to get a permalink to a particular area on the Open MapQuest site. This link option outputs only a shortened link. If you want a full link (to construct it yourself), apparently the URL GET parameters center and zoom work (example).

Or you can show the Open Mapquest map as a "layer" on the OSM main site and use the share button to generate a link (or copy the link in the browser toolbar).
