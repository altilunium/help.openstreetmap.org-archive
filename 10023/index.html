Find a junction of a similar type that as been mapped already, then open your editor and inspect what they have done.Then exit without saving. To add a lane or slip road is the same procedure of creating a node and drawing a way from that point. ask again if not sure. The editing videos are very good have a look at those too. http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Video_tutorials
