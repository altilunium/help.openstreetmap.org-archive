The main database is designed for editing and therefore indeed doesn't allow to export more than a very tiny piece of the map.

You can either download a Garmin map directly http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OSM_Map_On_Garmin/Download

or get the raw data from a mirror server http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Overpass_API/Language_Guide#Sample_map_calls

I guess you are using the export tab on osm.org. It simply may be that the area is still too big, yes. Furthermore, exports are very resource demanding and restricted more during high-load times.

You want an export for your Garmin? As far as I know you would not even be able to use the map formats offered by export in Garmins (at least not without processing on your own).

Download a MapSource map installer (+ possibly a pre-made gmapsupp.img for your device) from OSM_Map_On_Garmin/Download. See e.g. basic-guide-to-download-osm-to-garmin-gps and list of garmin questions for more info.
