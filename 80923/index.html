This might be worth a try...
Open a new layer in Josm, then select the route and ‘merge selected’ into the new layer. Then select all the route segments and ‘edit > combine’ and try exporting the combined route to gpx. Be careful not to upload to osm on exiting.

Also some alternative ideas in this query...
