It's not clear what you mean by "stuck to your cursor".

But I'm guessing that you have clicked on the node (red square) at the start or end of a way. If you do that, Potlatch assumes you want to extend the way, and starts drawing. If you don't, then just double-click or press Enter to finish drawing.
