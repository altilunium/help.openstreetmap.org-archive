The maps seen in Maps.me are offline maps and are typically updated only when the app itself is updated. This means you'll have to wait up to 2 weeks for new entries to appear in Maps.me. Note that OSM and Maps.me are separate entities. Maps.me merely builds its maps from OSM data.
