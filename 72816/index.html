That's caused be different tile update procedures for different zoom levels for openstreetmap.org. At zoom 12+ the data is updated pretty soon, for lower zooms it may take longer (up to a month).

The tiles at wmflabs.org did not update as well. I'm not even sure if they still will be updated as wikipedia moved to a different tile rendering stack and probalby don't support the old wmflabs.org tiles any more.
