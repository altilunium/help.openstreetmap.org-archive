With the exception of the "Standard" layer, the example maps on www.openstreetmap.org are provided by third parties. The creators and providers of the respective maps are responsible for the design and which features are included, any questions and suggestions should be directed to them. In the case of the transport map this would be Andy Allan, see http://www.thunderforest.com/
