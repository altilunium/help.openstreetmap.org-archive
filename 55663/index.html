For the charging station: see https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:amenity=charging_station (an additional bicycle=yes is suggested if it is also for E-bikes). For the single associated parking space you can use amenity=parking_space and capacity:charging=1. A (car) example: https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/3746157582 and https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/370957639 .
