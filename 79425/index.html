I am not aware of a way to add the check flag to an edit after the fact.

But there might be people who are specifically looking for changes in their area using one of a couple OSM tools.

For example, I use a few RSS feeds to monitor changes in the areas I am very interested in. If there is someone who is new to mapping or new to mapping in “my” area, then I look at their edits to see if I agree with them. You can get a list of some of the quality assurance tools that have RSS feeds at https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Feeds
