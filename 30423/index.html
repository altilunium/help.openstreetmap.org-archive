Most mappers would likely use name for the official name and loc_name for the local name. It's a grey area though, and at your (the mapper's) discretion - if you feel that the local name is so well established that somebody asking for directions to "Nathan Strong Park" would be met with a shrug, then maybe you should use name for the name that almost everyone knows, and record the official name as a side note under official_name.
