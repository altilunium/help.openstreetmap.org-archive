Ok thanks, I went with a bash script to do this :

for i in curl 'http://api.openstreetmap.org/api/0.6/relation/2418275' | grep stop | awk '{print $3}' | cut -d '"' -f2; do curl http://api.openstreetmap.org/api/0.6/node/${i} ; done`
