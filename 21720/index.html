@WalterStadt: you just need to wait. As long as your edit is saved in the data(!) it is fine. You can check your edits for that purpose if you are not sure. There is just one possible problem: if you mistyped some tag it will never appear anywhere in the map (even if the object type is usually shown in this specific map). However, if your edit is rendered at least on some zoom level then everything is fine obviously. Just wait for the other levels and clear your browser's cache before trying to look again.
