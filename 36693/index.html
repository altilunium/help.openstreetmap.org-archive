The format used by IGO seems to be proprietary and the conversion from OSM to it has to be performed by the manufacturer, at least there doesn't seem to be a free conversion program available.

You should likely contact http://www.igonavigation.com/map-updates-and-extras . See http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IGO_%28software%29 for more information.
