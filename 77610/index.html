Different maps get updated with differing frequencies. While the "standard" map on www.openstreetmap.org updates rather instantly the other maps may have considerably longer update cycles (up to a few days). Just be patient.

For viewing hiking routes you may be interested in the Waymarked Trails map. You can change the background layer to use Opentopomap which also shows reliefs.
