My first thought was community_centre but it seems more specialized than that.

  • If you're taking the word "wellness" at face value, healthcare=alternative seems good, though none of the specialties listed in the wiki seem to fit.
  • The website's emphasis on dance classes makes me think amenity=dancing_school, maybe with dance:style=belly_dancing;kundalini.
  • There are also yoga classes listed, which most people seem to tag as leisure=fitness_centre+sport=yoga.

If you're really into piling on tags, you could add all of these.

Sorry I don't have any mapped examples -- there's nothing on my radar in this particular grey area.

(There's also amenity=meditation_centre which seems to fit ok but it's very uncommon.)
