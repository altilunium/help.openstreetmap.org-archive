Licence terms changed slightly in April. You should now be looking at ODbl http://opendatacommons.org/licenses/odbl/summary/

Concerning the credits, I think either the tv channel should screen your video in its entirety (including your own credits with OSM) or if it is screening extracts along with other material, should incorporate the OSM credit into its own credits for the program.

This leaves the question of whether you created any "modified data" as part of the still image, which you might need to publish. But for anybody to comment on that, I think you would need to give more information about how that image would be created.
