Edits become live on the OpenStreetMap website instantly, they are then published as "diffs" to the planet file every minute. Users of OpenStreetMap data are not obligated to take updates as soon as they are available, some may update every few months, years, or never. If you would like to see an OpenStreetMap change somewhere else more quickly that their usual process (whatever that is) then you will need to contact that organisation.
