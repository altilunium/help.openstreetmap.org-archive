OpenStreetMap, well to be exact the OSMF, does not offer an aerial imagery layer. bing and mapbox/difitalglobe are kind enough to allow use of their aerial imagery for OSM tracing purposes, however that permission does not include other use (for example in an non-editing mobile app).

Both however offer comercial services that you may be able to use, please contact the resp. organisations for more information.

Note: if you do not require high resolution imagery there maybe other solutions.
