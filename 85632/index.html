power=generator, generator:source=solar, generator:type=solar_thermal_collector, generator:method=thermal, generator:output:hot_water=yes

Possibly we need an additional generator:type for domestic hot water systems as the wiki definition of solar_thermal_collector does not really encompass these. However, this is the widely used value.
