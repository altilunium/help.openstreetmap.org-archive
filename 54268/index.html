A guy on the talk-it list suggested to use the query down below, and it seems to properly work for me!

[out:xml][timeout:250][adiff:"2015-01-01T00:00:00Z","2016-01-01T00:00:00Z"];
(
way["highway"]({{bbox}});
);
out body;
out meta;
>;
out skel qt;

Thanks to Andrea Albani
