For tagging an office you should use the office key and for your specific case the lawyer value (currently the fifth popular value on taginfo). You can provide additional information by specifying name, addr and so on.

At the moment there is no special rendering for the office key on our main map as far as I know.

You want to include on your homepage a map with a marker, where the office is?

Choose map extent and zoom factor, then click on Export, under format choose HTML, under options click on "add marker", add marker where your office is by clicking on the map.

You will recieve an html-code which you can copy-paste into your website.
