My latest thoughts on this (https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:opening_hours)

barrier=swing_gate locked:conditional=yes opening_hours=Apr 01-Sep 31: Mo-Su 10:00-17:00, Oct 01-Mar 31: Mo-Su 10:00-16:00

Intended to mean - This is a gate it is UNLOCKED when the business is OPEN, and LOCKEDwhen the business is CLOSED
