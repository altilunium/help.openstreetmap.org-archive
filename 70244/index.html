A GPX file is a file that contains a series of track points of latitudes, longitudes and time stamps. For OSM the timestamps serve as a witness that they were recorded with a GPS or Smartphone carried by a mapper. A GPX can just be created over a map but that could be a copyright offence, and will not have times ( the times are are useful for speed, fitness and performance data as well of course). I occasionally open a GPX file with a text editor to repair one, If you did so you would see what they consist of.
see https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Upload_GPS_tracks
