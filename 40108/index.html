It depends solely on the specific classified use of the way... If it is classified as a pedestrian footway (highway=footway) and cycles are allowed add bicycle=yes (it will appear as a red dotted line) or... classified as a cycleway (highway=cycleway) and pedestrians are allowed add foot=yes (it will appear as a blue dotted line). It would be wise to check with the local council for that specific information.
