Roads in OpenStreetMap are classified by their importance in the road network and/or their intended use. Our wiki describes the different categories. In a general map this looks like this: https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=12/50.8469/24.3215.

You should describe a bit more what you intend to do. Do you need a onetime map of a specific area? Do you need a map that updates frequently? Is it something you want to share in the web or in an app?
