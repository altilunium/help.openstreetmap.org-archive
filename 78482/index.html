Hello, you have to map it! It's quite easy. The first thing you need to do is to browse to your location on OpenStreetMap.org on a desktop computer , choose Edit the map on top and follow the quick tutorial in the editor.

Then, for specifics, you can have a look at https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:piste:type%3Dnordic
