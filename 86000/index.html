Just to add on one of your other questions "¿Es posible agregar fotos del lugar?" OpenStreetMap does not store photos of any kind in its database. You can add contact details including social media profiles as a way of pointing to further information outside OSM, see these tags may be of interest: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:website https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Key:contact:*
