Since you use MapFactor you could have had a look how it works. Though I don't even have a smartphone I found out the following in less then a minute:

  • The app uses a proprietary vector data format.
  • This kind of data you cannot download from osm.org (which is no place to get end-user data from anyway).
  • Since there seems no according software to convert OSM data yourself you have to rely solely on mapfactor for data updates.
  • Which time the last update was made you can check at the mapfactor site.
  • How often maps normally are updated you can read here in the middle of the thread though e.g. the map for Italy (have a look at the previous link ) is from the end of December.
