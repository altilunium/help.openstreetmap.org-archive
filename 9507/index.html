Are you trying to import some data from a list of points of interest that you know the latitude and longitude of, or do you have a GPS trace in which is recorded the latitude and longitude of the points that you want to add?

If the latter, then you can upload the GPS trace to OSM (including any WPT waypoints contained within it) and then convert those waypoints directly into OSM nodes in the Potlatch 2 editor via "alt-click". You'll need to add any required tags to nodes that you create in this way of course.
