Yes and no.

If you mean "the" map displayed at the main openstreetmap.org site, there is the possibility of designing a new shop icon and then filing an issue with the openstreetmap-mapnik stylesheet (via https://github.com/openstreetmap/mapnik-stylesheets/issues ).

Also, you could build a website yourself where you display an OSM view with OpenLayers and overlay that with your shop icons.
