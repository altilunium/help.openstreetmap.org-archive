I have been fooled a couple of times by these effects. Is the gps enabled and indicating enough satellites to locate, outdoors? and is it in the same area as the map, you could get the device to display a lat lon to check. Secondly what does the scale show when in map view? you may be zoomed into an empty area or zoomed out so far the the map is so small it cannot be seen. If you have loaded a map for an area you are not in at the moment you could create a waypoint that is in the area of the map and display that, then see if zooming in or out to a sensible scale solves the problem.
