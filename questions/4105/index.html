In potlatch 2, select one of the polygons, choose the advanced tab for tagging at the bottom left of the screen (shortcut T). Then click the "add to" button at the bottom of the relation section, choose new relation, select "advanced->multipolygon" from the dropdown. Close the "edit relation" box. Now set the role to inner or outer in the left pane.

Check the id of your new relation, then select the other polygon you want to add to the relation, choose "add to" again and select your multipolygon relation. Set the role for this one also. Now you are done.
