Hi, Welcome to OpenStreetMap. There's a general help button at the top of your weblink page which leads to lots of help.

As you refer to a link to the iD editor perhaps the best help to direct you to is here:- https://learnosm.org/en/beginner/id-editor/ Have a read there, then have a go at adding something, check that your data is correct before uploading it.
