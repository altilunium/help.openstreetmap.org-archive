You can't edit a changeset comment but you can add comments to a changeset if you want, for example to correct mistakes. It's likely only of interest to humans readers though, it's not likely to get picked up for automated project stats.

Go to your profile on osm.org, click "my edits" and then the relevant changeset, then use the "discussion" box.
