Hi, each tile on each zoom level has to be re-rendered every time you zoom in or out, or (if you're zoomed in close) when you pan to a onto another tile (bring another tile into view). So the capability of your device will determine the speed of re-render. My machine takes two or three seconds, it sometimes hangs up for ten to twenty seconds.
