If a Flickr photo was linked to an OSM item (for example, as street-level imagery), then a deleted photo may no longer serve a purpose in OSM. Sometimes Wikimedia Commons imports appropriately-licensed photos to it's repository (https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/category:files_from_Flickr), where they may remain even after the original is deleted, but there are no guarantees.
