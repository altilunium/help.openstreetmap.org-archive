The correct tag would be boundary=protected_area, protect_class=5. On the wiki, see the table for nature-protected areas, and scroll down to the US section.

I'm not sure where exactly the tag should go though--on the river way? riverbank? does the Wild & Scenic protection include area outside the riverbank?
