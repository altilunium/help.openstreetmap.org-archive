Nothing. As yet there isn't a simple way of turning the traces off in P2 (though I think you're mistaken when you say that P1 does that; the difference is that P1 wipes out the previous load when you pan to a new area and load tracks, whereas P2 doesn't).

However, for now, if you do want to turn them off you can do it this way:

  • Go to 'Vector file...' in the Background menu
  • Select the 'GPS tracks' entry
  • Click 'Delete'
