With https://simon04.dev.openstreetmap.org/whodidit/?zoom=15&lat=43.93315&lon=-111.75499&layers=BTT&age=6%20month we find:

  • The city was removed on 4th June ( https://www.openstreetmap.org/changeset/59522317 ). It was a node with tags name=round about and place=city
  • Another attempt on 21th June because it still shows up on the rendered map ( https://www.openstreetmap.org/changeset/60056446 ; note that the removed objects very very very unlikely are to blame: just buildings and a surveillance camera, ...)

I had a look with https://overpass-turbo.eu/ with a scope of the whole North America area and could not find any other object there with the name "round about". (well, that one: https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/471941229 , which is a street round about on Jamaica island)

So, we have to wait for the update process of those zoom levels. See https://help.openstreetmap.org/questions/178/how-often-does-the-main-mapnik-map-get-updated and also kocio's (18 Dec '17, 03:59) comment there : first Sunday of each month is the next update of the low zoom tiles. That would be 1st July. Last one was 3rd June which is why the last update did not remove the wrong label.

The z12 tile is https://a.tile.openstreetmap.org/12/776/1490.png (via right click on https://mc.bbbike.org/mc map). Before its status: "Tile is clean. Last rendered at Mon Jun 04 03:37:19 2018."

I have appended /dirty to its URL to request re-rendering. That made it update before the schedule. Now it is this status: "Tile is clean. Last rendered at Fri Jun 29 05:07:32 2018. ". And the label is gone. I did so for z11-z6 too. Already (after not more than 2 minutes) all wrong labels are gone.
