Also note that editing the map yourself is fast and easy (much more so than with Google Maps). Just click "edit" at the top of the page, fix the road shape or name (dont copy from Google or other copyrighted sources), and click "save". The modification will appear on the rendered map within minutes. See also http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Beginner's_Guide .
