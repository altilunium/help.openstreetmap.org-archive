Routing engines are listed at Routing (and sub/linked pages) in our wiki. Likely some may also be used for indoor routing or could be adapted for. If you found something, please mention it here for future reference.

Also there is Indoor Mapping described in general. Note that indoor mapping is very very incomplete in our data and experimental.
