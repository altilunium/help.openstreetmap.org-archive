Your data becomes part of the OSM database as soon as you upload it. The review_requested tag doesn't change that - it flags to other mappers that you would like them to look at your changes, but those changes have already been made in the database.

I can see that changeset, so your changes were saved/uploaded, but I don't know whether the map reflects what you expected to see. If you can link to a specific object, for example in the format https://www.openstreetmap.org/way/542012134 , and describe what you feel is wrong we may be able to help more.
