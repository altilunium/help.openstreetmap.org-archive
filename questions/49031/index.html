I would use multipolygons to map this, which are able to model areas with holes.

In your example, there seem to be some buildings(?) together with the steps in the center of the pedestrian area. Those should all be excluded from the pedestrian area by using a multipolygon. You can then connect the steps to the inner ring of the multipolygon with a shared node.
