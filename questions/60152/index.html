If such data is in OSM it would be a very small amount, the majority of the buildings in Puerto Rico are either from imports that did not include such information or from HOT activity related to Hurricane Maria.

There is a data portal: https://data.pr.gov/data

And cadastral data with building footprints: https://www.satasgis.crimpr.net/cdprpc/ (turn on the "Estructuras" layer), but the metadata shown is limited to the building height.
