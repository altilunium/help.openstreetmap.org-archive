Not adding the shop number to the name was certainly the right decision.

Many people use the generic ref tag for different kinds of numbering. If you want to go over the top you can invent a ref:home_depot tag (just in case someone later wants to add, say, a ref: fire_department or a ref:irs to the thing as well...). Details on the wiki.
