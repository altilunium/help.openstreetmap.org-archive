From your recent edits I see Riera de Cabrils is an example of your problem. In this case, it looks like changing it from waterway=stream to waterway=river was reasonable. OpenCycleMap will render it much wider with waterway=river; you just need to wait a few days for OpenCycleMap to be updated.

For narrower streams, which need waterway=stream, that's just how the OpenCycleMap developer decided to render them. You can try filing a request to see whether he feels like changing it.
