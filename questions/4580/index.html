A general answer about Mapnik:

If you edit the map, it will take a while before the changes become visible on the Mapnik layer. Also, different tiles & different magnfications may update at different times, so things may look funny for a few days.

See e.g. this question http://help.openstreetmap.org/questions/1049/how-to-trigger-a-repaint-for-a-specific-osm-map-tile for more information.

Edit:

Looking at the permalink above, yes, it does look like a simple Mapnik update problem. The mapping and tagging looks ok, and the Osmarender layer shows the river correctly (the Osmarender layer is typically update more frequently than the Mapnik layer). So the problem will likely go away in a few days or so.
