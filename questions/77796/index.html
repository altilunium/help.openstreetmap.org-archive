Wikimedia stopped allowing third-party websites like yours to use the map tiles they provide at maps.wikimedia.org. That's why you get a 403 error.

So either use the openstreetmap.org tiles while respecting the OSM tile usage polices (SomeoneElse posted the link already) or look out for a tile provider here: https://switch2osm.org/providers/
