In iD there are some pre-set filters that you can use:

  1. Expand the Map data panel using the second button in the second group of buttons on the right hand side of the map area.
  2. Expand the "Map Features" list.
  3. Deselect categories you don't want to see.

Try to remember not to re-add things you have hidden.
