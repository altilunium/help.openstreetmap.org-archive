You can download GPX routes from https://waymarkedtrails.org.

  • Go to https://cycling.waymarkedtrails.org
  • Enter "Greenway Praha-Wien" into the search field and select your cycle route from the results -> https://cycling.waymarkedtrails.org/#route?id=108494
  • Click on "Show on map" to verify if it matches your expectations
  • Click on "GPX" to download the track
