You should be more specific, OpenStreetMap data can be used in various ways.

If you want to use it on a Garmin GPS device, you should take a look at the wiki page about available maps for Garmin devices. You might also be interested in other things you can do with Garmin devices.

There is also a list about desktop software and mobile devices you can use with OSM.

Furthermore there are lots of other possibilities, like rendering your own maps or just analyzing the data of a specific area.
