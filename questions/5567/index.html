In Potlatch 2, a gas station is indicated the the small gas pump icon in the "Transport" category.

In JOSM, follow the Presets » Transport » Car » Fuel, also indicated by a small gas pump icon.

If you are doing things the manual way, use key:amenity; value:fuel to add a gas station to OpenStreetMap.
