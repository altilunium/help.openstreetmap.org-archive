My edit from about 4 days ago shows up in MapQuest Open already (high zoom levels).

Keep in mind that …

  • some feature classes (tags) may not be rendered by MapQuest,
  • you may need to circumvent your browser's cache,
  • only a request to MapQuest triggers a re-rendering, so you may need to reload again (without cache).
  • In general, see also https://help.openstreetmap.org/tags/update/?sort=mostvoted
