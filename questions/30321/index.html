Go to www.openstreetmap.org and zoom in on your neighbourhood. Then use the button on the right to place a "note", where you can write a few words that describe the mistake (e.g. "this house has address X" or so).

This will not automatically fix the map but allow mappers to see clearly what is wrong and fix it manually.
