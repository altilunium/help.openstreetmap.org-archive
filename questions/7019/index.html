If you just want to share a URL:

  1. Centre and zoom the map however you want.
  2. Press the "Permalink" button at the bottom right
  3. Share the URL that's now in your browser address bar.

That'll be a URL something like:

http://www.openstreetmap.org/?lat=47.5433&lon=-52.8734&zoom=12&layers=M

If you like, you can change "lat" to "mlat" and "lon" to "mlon" to get a marker at the spot:

www.openstreetmap.org/?mlat=47.5433&mlon=-52.8734&zoom=12&layers=M
