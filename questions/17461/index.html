OpenStreetMap has a focus on data collection, not map creation. The map on the openstreetmap.org main site tries to showcase some of the data, but it can’t possibly show everything or fulfill every need. There are lots of maps and projects based on OSM data. Maybe some of them already fulfill your need?

What about e.g. AmauroMap or the toner map?

If those don’t help you I’m afraid you will have to find someone or an organization to create a high-contrast map style for you e.g. in TileMill.
