On the Standard Mapnik map on openstreetmap.org, the coastline can take a while to update. Most edits show up within a few minutes or hours, but changes to the coastline can take weeks or months to appear.

See this previous question: Why do the changes I have made to coastline not appear on the map?

Your edits to the coastline and beach appear to be correct, you just have to wait a while for them to appear on the map.
