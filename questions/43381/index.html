As you wrote, the Bing aerial images are only available during edit. Your friend is probably looking at the area on openstreetmap.org in "view"-mode. We have the rights to use Bing images to trace, but that's it. Openstreetmap.org has no functionality to show arial images. And as far as I know there are no plans to add this functionality.
