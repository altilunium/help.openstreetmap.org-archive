Scanning a map and copying things into OpenStreetMap is normally a breach of copyright. If it's an out-of-copyright old map, or released to the public domain, then that's OK.

There are various tools and approaches you could take. If it's not a gigantic scanned map image, then have a look at Geothings Map Warper, which makes it easy to add control points and then load warped images into JOSM as a WMS.
