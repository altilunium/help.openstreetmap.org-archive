Many maps have a 'Permalink' link you can click to give you a URL which you can share. osm.org also has a 'Shortlink' option.

Also, with the Permalink option on osm.org, you can manually modify the URL to have mlat= and mlon= instead of lat= and lon=, and this will place a marker at the specific location.

-- Steve
