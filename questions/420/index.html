  1. Browse to where a member of the relation is on the map
  2. Click the blue/white '+' sign on the right-hand side of the map and check the 'Data' box (make sure you're zoomed all the way in).
  3. Click on the relation member in the map view and then click the 'Details' link in the box on the left.
  4. This will open a page describing the way. At the bottom of it, there will be a list of all the relations this way is a member of. Clicking on the name of the relation in that list will take you to the relation description page.
  5. On this page there is a map on the right-hand side under which are two links. Clicking on the 'View relation on larger map' will take you to a full-screen OSM map with the relation highlighted (something like this).

To export this map, just use the standard exporting methods (screenshot or the 'Export' tab) or use the 'Permalink' link at the bottom left.

Don't forget that embedding an OSM map in Wikipedia could potentially generate vast amounts of traffic which OSM is not funded to cater for. You should investigate using Wikimedia-owned servers for this. http://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/OpenStreetMap is a starting point.
