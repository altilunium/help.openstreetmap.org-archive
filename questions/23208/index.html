Maybe you need to update the map on your device.

Maybe the details got fixed just recently and Navdroyd did not update the data yet. You could look when a detail was last changed in the OSM data with the data browser (if you have problems, please describe which detail you mean and paste a permalink of http://www.openstreetmap.org in a comment here and someone will tell you).

Maybe the Navdroyd map is created differently ("rendered") than the standard map on osm.org - it may interpret our data differently or may not show some kinds of data at all. This ''may'' be caused by (partly) incorrect/uncommonly tagged OSM data. Again: show us a example "error" location and "error" description and we can have a look and ''may'' fix our data (side note: we will not change the data if the only reason would be to let it appear on the Navdroyd map).

I don't know if you can insert external maps into Navdroyd - but, as alternative, you could use another Anrdoid app which uses OSM data.
