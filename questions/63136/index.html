There are naturally more than one way you could do this. However the easiest is likely to use overpass-api queries to get the indoor and display that. You won't get a layer switcher or similar though.

I expect that re-using OpenLevelUp (https://openlevelup.net https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OpenLevelUp https://framagit.org/OpenLevelUp/OpenLevelUp) in some form might be more appropriate.
