It turns out that I had not put the resource files (icons and *.xml files) in the right subdirectories. I thought I followed the directions at: http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Potlatch_2/Deploying_Potlatch_2 but when I looked at the zgroks site in the Google Chrome JavaScript console, I saw many error messages. Moved the files to the indicated directories and it works: http://www.zgroks.com/potlatch/potlatch2.html. Kewl!
