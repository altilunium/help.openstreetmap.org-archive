By now this won't help the OP, but for others like me who stumbled upon this unanswered question, there might be hope.

  1. If the feature was previously saved and you deleted it, then just hit CANCEL instead of SAVE at the top.
  2. In my case, I deleted something that had been saved before and hit SAVE on accident. I got it back by restoring a previous version of the layer by going to Manage Layers (stack of discs on right-hand sidebar)> Edit (pencil next to desired layer)> Versions and the clicking on the list of dates that appears.

If you created a feature and deleted it without ever saving it, I don't know of anything that can be done.
