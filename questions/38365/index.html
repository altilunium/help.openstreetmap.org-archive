You can't buy tiles from OpenStreetMap (we don't provide paid-for services) and the Tile Usage Policy expressly prohibits you downloading them all.

You may be able to find a commercial provider who will do this for you. The list at switch2osm.org/providers is a good start. Bear in mind that "all the tiles" - every zoom level for the whole world - would require an unimaginably vast amount of storage.

Alternatively, you can download the data from OSM and create the tiles yourself. Again, switch2osm.org is the best resource for getting started.
