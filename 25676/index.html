If you go to our log in page, and then to "forgot password" you get to: http://www.openstreetmap.org/user/forgot-password There you can enter the email address you used to register. You can then login with the email address and the new password. The login name is not required.

You also can find out your user name if you look in the data history of objects you have edited before (e.g. by using data layer).

Please tell us here if it worked.

If all of that does not work you could contact the admins (maybe they can help), or you could just edit with a new account.
