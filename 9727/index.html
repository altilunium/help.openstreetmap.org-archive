Instead of using the Export tab on the OSM page to get .osm data, you can use a Planet extract. These are available for a variety of countries/areas, covering most of the world. See Planet.osm

If you don't need all of the area of a Planet extract, you can use Osmosis or Osmconvert to extract a smaller area, before loading it into Mapwel. Or use Splitter to split it into smaller tiles.
