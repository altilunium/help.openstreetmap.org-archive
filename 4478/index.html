You can remove the tag altogether. That the road came from TIGER originally is visible from other tags if anyone should be interested.

There are no hard and fast rules about what constitutes a "review" but if you have satisfied yourself (using aerial imagery and local knowledge) that nothing is obviously wrong, I reckon the road is as good as anything manually mapped, so don't be shy. "Reviewed" does certainly not mean "guaranteed 100% correct".
