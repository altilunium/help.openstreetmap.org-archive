You probably typed [ALT]+Z by mistake.

The building tool has several modes, one of which is aimed at circular buildings.

[CTRL]+[ALT]+B will open the preferences panel to switch back to rectangle mode. Alternatively you can use [ALT]+R or the corresponding item in the Data menu.

More information is available on the wiki.
