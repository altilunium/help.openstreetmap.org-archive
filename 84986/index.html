This doesn't answer the question, but the best I've found is this:

  1. Click the Hamburger Menu to open the settings
  2. Click "Configure Map"
  3. At the top of the new menu, change from the "globe" tab to the "person-walking" tab. This changes the profile from "Browse map" to "Walking Profile"
  4. Scroll down and enable "Hiking routes"
  5. Scroll down and click "Details" -> Enable "More Details"

In my experience, this will show the trails at three (3) more zoomed-out zoom levels.

However, this doesn't buy you much. I'm not sure how to make trails more visible at all zoom levels.
