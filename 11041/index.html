If the footpaths are for the public to use there is not much different from a legal right of way. You can add an operator=* tag to the footpaths to show that these paths are operated by the local voluntary group.

For footpaths that aren't designated as footpaths, bridleways, byways etc. I personally wouldn't use "designation" - I'd tag the access as accurately as possible (which might be "yes", "permissive" or perhaps something else), and if relevant add some sort of note explaining the situation.
