See this question: https://help.openstreetmap.org/questions/864/how-to-add-node-with-exact-coordinates-in-josm .

JOSM is the java openstreetmap editor available from http://josm.openstreetmap.org

Also, be sure you have WGS84 coordinates and not Gauss-Krüger or UTM.

From your spelling of "projekt" I infer you are germany-based, where coordinates are given in Gauss-Krüger or, recently, UTM.
