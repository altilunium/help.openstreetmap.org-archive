(sorry, not much time now)

Welcome! :-) Your changes will appear magically after some time. The update process just takes some time. Please the top questions at see https://help.openstreetmap.org/tags/update/?sort=mostvoted and https://help.openstreetmap.org/tags/not_shown/?sort=mostvoted . Your changes to the coastline take much longer (weeks), that is normal - see https://help.openstreetmap.org/questions/276/why-do-the-changes-i-have-made-to-coastline-not-appear-on-the-map .

Other contributors (like you) will, hopefully, look at your changeset if you requested review. If that happens depends if there are enough active people. Yes, you can do reviews, too: just add a changeset discussion comment with your feedback. However, I would not regard this as very newbie-compatible task. :-) See https://help.openstreetmap.org/tags/review_requested/ and http://neis-one.org/2017/09/review-requests-osm/ .

If you have some specific other questions, please ask them all on separate question entries on this site.

Happy mapping!
