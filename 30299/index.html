If you got it from http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/WikiProject_Europe/EuroVelo then it wont help you at all. Those xml files only show some osm tags and relation numbers. On http://cycling.waymarkedtrails.org you can download gpx tracks but the longer the route, the more fragmented and unsorted those tracks are, and of limited use for your Garmin.
