You're filtering out "the rest" by restricting the returned data with (user:"x")

You need to out the meta data.

[bbox:{{bbox}}];
way[highway];
node(w);
out meta;

{{style:
node{color:blue;fill-color:blue;symbol-size:3;}
node[@user=X] {color:red;fill-color:red;symbol-size:5;}
}}
