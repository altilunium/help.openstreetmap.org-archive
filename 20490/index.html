...if there are no buildings yet in the area (see @SomeoneElse 's comment), maybe there will be some nice people willing to trace those airport buildings for you.

OpenStreetMap is a volunteer project. If nobody has yet drawn the buildings for your area of interest, they are simply not in the database.

For a quick introduction on how to add stuff to the OSM database see http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Beginners%27_guide and especially http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Potlatch/Primer .

And be bold, it's not as hard as you may think!
