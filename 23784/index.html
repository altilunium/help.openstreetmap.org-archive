  • How to create an embdedded map showing a route
  • In our wiki there is a list of track drawing / uploading websites (you could make a screenshot; some allow/offer embedding - please check the usage policy, especially if your site is not just a small personal homepage)
  • Look at those previous questions for "private"/custom maps
  • And, last but not least, OpenStreetMap is basically just data - and, contrary to Google Maps, nearly everything is possible (as long as you comply to our license but you maybe need to program it on your own or pay someone). There is a list of already existing OSM-based services.
