See https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Coastline#Rendering_in_Standard_tile_layer_on_openstreetmap.org

You can request a single tile to be re-rendered by adding '/dirty' to its URL in your browser. Admittedly a bit cumbersome.
