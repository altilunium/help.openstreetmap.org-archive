Discontinuities in imagery mosaics due to different sources / dates / equipment / orthonormalization etc, are completely normal and it is just bad luck when this happens in the middle of a city.

Solutions can be:

  • using a different imager source (as the answer say)
  • realigning the imagery depending on which side is being used (a bit of a pita),
