According to tag info there are 2 natural=cairn, and couple of cairn=yes (see http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/keys/cairn#values), the most popular is man_made=cairn (1517 times) and landmark=cairn (73).

I would therefore go for man_made=cairn (http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:man_made%3Dcairn)
