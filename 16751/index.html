Navfree is software that uses OpenStreetMap data. This help site is typically for OpenStreetMap itself.

Normally, you'll have the best chance for support/help by visiting the author's website. Navfree's is http://www.navmii.com/support/faq#q9

The answer to your question is found on their website: "The setting is available from the International Preferences menu Menu -> Advanced -> Preferences -> International In the further updates this menu item will be renamed to "Distance Units" "
