I guess the nominatim reverse-geocoding for http://www.openstreetmap.org/node/2648597457 is meant. This is displayed via a search for "Free wi-fi Palazzo Barbieri":

Free wi-fi Palazzo Barbieri, Piazza Bra, Quinzano, Verona, VR, Veneto, 37125, Italy

This is also visible at http://nominatim.openstreetmap.org/details.php?place_id=9161955595

I guess (I am really not a Nominatim expert) this may be because there are place=suburb outside Verona, but not (more near) inside Verona. And the next suburb to the Palazzo node is the one named "Quinzano". So nominatim uses this suburb as a refinement of the location description. Maybe there are also some boundary relations not correctly mapped.

It is not a problem for you, linda, just do not care about it now. It is not related to your edit. Please better ask such highly specific questions in our forum or chat next time.
