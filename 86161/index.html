It takes some time to render a new feature at all zoom levels.

Routing/navigation are all performed by separate projects or companies and it takes some time for them to update their databases.

End result: Don't expect your changes to be immediately visible or usable in routing for at least a few days and maybe even a month or more (for some navigation apps).
