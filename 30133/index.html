First: Welcome to OSM! You already did great steps toward fixing the issue! Thank you!

I guess the street problem is also on http://www.openstreetmap.org/ right? Not only on OsmAnd?

This help site is meant for quite general problems and not for collaborative mapping. On this help site you can find how to fix it yourself (you could also see Beginners' guide or learnosm.org). You can also ask specific, but general problems on why you cannot fix it yourself (editor problems?).

I think it would be best to go to http://forum.openstreetmap.org/viewforum.php?id=74 (your osm.org login works) and add your question there. You can then discuss (questions my be needed to map it correctly) with people from Brazil what is wrong and how it should be improved.

Other options would be a mailing list post or a note on www.osm.org with a description and link to your trace.
