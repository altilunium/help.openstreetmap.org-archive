The process that works for me on Android is exactly as described in @miller's answer:

  • Login to osm.org
  • Click my username at the top of the screen to see the profile
  • Click "my settings"
  • Scroll down
  • Tick the box that says "Update home location when I click on the map"
  • Tap the map. The marker moves.
  • Click "Save changes"
  • A message "User information updated successfully" is displayed.
