If you are looking at https://www.openstreetmap.org/ you will not see any ski trails. They simply cannot render everything that is mapped so they render what they think most people would like to see.

For ski trails look at other renderers like the one at Open Snow Map: http://opensnowmap.org

If I recall correctly, for portable offline use OsmAnd can display ski trails (it might need a plug-in/add-on for this).
