If a place has names in other languages they will typically be present in the OSM data and it depends solely on your application if they will be displayed or not.

The "standard" map style simply uses what is in the "name" tag, for example the MapQuest Open style uses English names where present.

In other words it depends on what MapFactor does with the data.
