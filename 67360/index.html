I suspect the access=private tag will cause many/most/all routers decide that it can't be used. Access=destination might make a router more likely to assume it can be used to reach the destination.

Be advised that each router project grabs snapshots of the OSM data at intervals that they choose. So your mapping changes may not be picked up by your chosen router for quite some time (maybe months).
