It seems as if the city district can only be found if you search for: Кировский район, Каза́нь

see: https://nominatim.osm.org/search.php?q=%D0%9A%D0%B8%D1%80%D0%BE%D0%B2%D1%81%D0%BA%D0%B8%D0%B9+%D1%80%D0%B0%D0%B9%D0%BE%D0%BD%2C+%D0%9A%D0%B0%D0%B7%D0%B0%CC%81%D0%BD%D1%8C&format=json

As nominatim cannot expand the район and the district is tagged with Кировский район, it does not find the district while just searching for Кировский.

You could add an alt_name of Кировский to the Кировский район relation here: https://www.openstreetmap.org/relation/2133463
