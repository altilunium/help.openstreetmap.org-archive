Go to osm.org, zoom in to the tunnel, and click the "edit" button to enter the Potlach editor. Then click on the tunel, and edit its tags on the left-hand side (choose "advanced" view at the bottom of the panel if your tag doesn't show up). See the beginner's guide for more info. Welcome :)
