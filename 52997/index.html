I can't see any sidewalks tagged to any of these streets. That should be the main problem here. Further some of the footpaths end without being connected to any other street, that's probably not like that in reality. Have a look at https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Sidewalks and add some after reading the instructions, because adding sidewalks and footpaths in OSM is kind of complicated sometimes :)
