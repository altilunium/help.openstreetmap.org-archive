@bdaventura: yes, that matters, sorry. We cannot use phonebook's data. See https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Legal_FAQ#2._Contributing and https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sui_generis_database_right . Regarding "better": if we would just reuse bad data, be cannot get better. Going out and really looking which name is there makes us better.
