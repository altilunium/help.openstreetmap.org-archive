highway=service is clearly wrong, that's correct. I would go for highway=path since the trails are "not big enough for a tractor or four wheel vehicle". A track requires to be passable by a typical four-wheeled vehicle which isn't possible for these trails.

Regarding the bad quality of these trails I'm not aware of any established tagging scheme. I guess you are referring to conditions more worse than in your photo. I would consider a few obstacles from time to time pretty normal for a path.
