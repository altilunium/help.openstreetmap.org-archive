Hi iii, it all depends on the use of the building, you’ll have to go out to survey, the Wiki stated hut or shed, like you suggested too. A shed looks bigger then a hut. My first choice building=hut, based on a crude and simple structure, which offers 'shelter' for humans and tools. Most buildings grow in time, unless there’s a regulated board with garden rules what should or not. Greenhouse is linked to bigger structures, although some gardeners build them as a shed.
