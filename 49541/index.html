You need to be logged in the wiki first (top right). Then there is a "Upload file" (or "Datei hochladen" in German) in the "tools" box on the left side panel.

If you still cannot see this link there, then maybe your account is too new. Your wiki account must be 4 days old and have 10 wiki edits to be autoconfirmed
