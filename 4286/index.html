Please, separate your question in two.

First question: Yes, you can create an Android app that uses OSM data as far as you complain the OSM legal terms (See http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Legal_FAQ)

Second question: Yes, you can extract the area for one specific country. See: http://help.openstreetmap.org/questions/713/how-to-extract-partial-data-for-large-regions
