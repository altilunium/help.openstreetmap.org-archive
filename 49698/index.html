Hi Johnny, Many gardens are just marked with a boundary, even the famous Keukenhof has one border, http://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=15/52.2695/4.5406 and none of the flower beds is marked individually. Your question is almost the same as this problem, the exact border or some that looks like as a question passed some time ago, https://help.openstreetmap.org/questions/49606/mapping-park-boundaries-de-facto-or-de-jure
I would go for a less correct border (de-jure) and specify the individual patches with the actually species growing there, using the already present multipolygone.
