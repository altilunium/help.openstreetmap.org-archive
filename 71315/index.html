Based on the pharmacy example on this page I tried 

[out:json][timeout:25];
(
  node["highway"="bus_stop"]({{bbox}});
  way["highway"="bus_stop"]({{bbox}});
  relation["highway"="bus_stop"]({{bbox}});
);
out count;

which for the area selected returned
