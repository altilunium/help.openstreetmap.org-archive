This "image size" means that the coordinates, at the given scale, make for an 888 pixel by 845 pixel image.

There is no way (with the export tab) to request exactly 600 by 600 pixels in size (but there might be with some of the Static Maps scripts), nor is there a way (with the export tab) to say "i want 800 by 800 metres" but if you have your coordinates right then it will give you that area on the ground.
