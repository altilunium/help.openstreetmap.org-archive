Please see https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Platform_Status "Performance degraded until about 5th August (server move ongoing)".

Further, please note that the free service at https://www.openstreetmap.org (and its standard tiles) is driven solely by volunteers and donations. This service is primarily meant for contributing community members. There is no SLA. If you need that in your offices (and if you have a high usage volume!) please switch to a professional map service using OSM data.

Your offices are in the US, so this is likely related: OSM_tech team wrote on 1. Aug. 2018: "The tile.openstreetmap.org cluster has been over capacity in North America for the last few days. Anyone able to offer a high-traffic server in North America?"
