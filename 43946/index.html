Please see the wiki page about JTileDownloader. Scraping tiles from OSM's tile servers is against their tile usage policy. Therefore JTileDownloader has been blocked.

There are various alternatives:

  • Download tiles from a different tile server, for example the ones provided by MapQuest. They usually have less strict usage policies.
  • Render your own tiles, for example by using TileMill or Maperitive
  • Choose a commercial tile provider.
