I can't speak for what's "proper" in Canada, but for the U.S., when putting addr:city or other city tags on things, I just use the city name: Atlanta, Miami, etc.

The exception is when I want to specify the city government for something, such as in the operator tag; then I use "City of ....". I would tag city courts or the police with "operator:City of Marietta" or "operator:City of Atlanta"

Hi Mike, welcome there has been such a kind of question before, http://forum.openstreetmap.org/viewtopic.php?id=7894 one remark, the Canadian community looks rather calm at the asking side, but asking could have some effect anyway, there a lot of viewers. OSM does not encourage automated mass corrections or better denotes them. It’s a risky business with the side effect of unwanted changes. Try to mail the original mapper by using the history button and ask why the name tag is as it is. Please ask the city council of Ottawa how the name should be written out officially and change the name at the official and administrative borders if necessary. Read the beginner’s site as well, http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Main_Page and the beginner’s site.
