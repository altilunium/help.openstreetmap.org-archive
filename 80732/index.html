Would it happen to be of similar width and accessibility, just closed to cars with legal restrictions or removable barriers? In that case it would be a =pedestrian instead, as a pedestrianized vehicular street.

In any case, split the road at the change, then change it to the appropriate one. Don't delete and re-add.
