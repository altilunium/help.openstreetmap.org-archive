[[URL|{{image URL}}]]

e.g.

[[https://www.openstreetmap.org|{{https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b0/Openstreetmap_logo.svg/240px-Openstreetmap_logo.svg.png}}]]
