Always use the full or official name (Rua NNN), otherwise we won't know if it's a Rua, Avenida, Travessa, Beco, etc.

If the street is locally known by other names you can use alt_name, short_name, loc_name, etc (see http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Name)

If the app is including or inferring something that doesn't exist then it's a problem with the app (and not in OSM data).
