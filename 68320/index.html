I can see the logic in moving "abandoned" to a lifecycle tag on the key - so that you can see what sort of highway it was that was abandoned. Personally, I suspect that either tagging should work, because anyone going out of their way to show abandoned highways on a map or otherwise process the data isn't going to just read the wiki; they're going to look at the data in OSM and ask how people have represented that particular feature.
