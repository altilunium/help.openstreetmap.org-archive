Currently there are tags that describe the cell towers http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/User:!i!/telecommunication but on the ground you don't see the MNC/LAC so we can't tag them.

What you are looking for are projects like the following to lookup GSM cell ids:

  • http://www.opencellid.org
  • http://www.openbmap.org
  • https://location.services.mozilla.com/

P.S: Please be aware that this forum is dedicated to OSM only and we can't give support to other projects.
