The application does not automatically load new data from OSM. Instead, the maker of the applications needs to build new map packages (OSM does not control how frequently that happens) and then you need to download such a package. You were right in contacting support@maps.me but perhaps your question to them should simply be "how do I update my maps".

To add on Frederik Ramm's answer, Maps.me updates its downloadable maps only when releasing new versions of the app. So you'll have to wait for a notification to update the app, and then in Maps.me you'll need to download new maps. Of course at the time you do that, the data extracted from OSM might already be somewhat old, so you might have to wait for a second cycle of updates.

Other app have various policy, for example OsmAnd has monthly updates of maps for the free version, and up to hourly updates for the paid version, new version of the app or not.

Otherwise there is a fork of Maps.me, "Organic Maps", that has the same policy as Maps.me, but release new versions of the app quite more often, and they want to switch to separate update frequencies at some point.

Hope this helps.
