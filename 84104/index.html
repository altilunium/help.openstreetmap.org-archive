Yes but making art does not absolve you from the attribution requirements, so you must ensure that people interacting with your art have a chance to learn that it uses OpenStreetMap data. See https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Licence/Licence_and_Legal_FAQ#What_do_you_mean_by_%22Attribution%22? for more.
