From the error message you mention, can we assume you are indoors ? In such case you might get the error that no satellites can be found. Correctly installed maps will still be shown in such case (at least on my Garmin Dakota). The map will probably be positioned on the last spot where you got satellite reception. (Which is somewhere in Canada ?)

Can you move the map to Costa Rica and zoom in ? Do you see roads there ?

For me the error message has nothing to do with the Costa Rica map. For a correct installation look at SomeoneElse comment
