Unless you find something better, I would go for this:

  • waterway=flow_control
  • flow_control=moench

But what about the tags flow_control=overflow or flow_control=water_level from http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/keys/flow_control#values ?

In such rare tagging cases I like to add a description (possibly just your Wikipedia link) tag, too.
