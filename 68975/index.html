Objects with craft=* tags are not specifically rendered on the standard OpenStreetMap-Carto style. Your node does show up on the map, as "8570", its house number. If the brewery sells beer for consumption on the premises then I guess it would be acceptable to add an amenity=pub tag.

Potential future rendering of craft icons is discussed here https://github.com/gravitystorm/openstreetmap-carto/pull/3126 and apparently is looking for someone to work on it.
