After checking the different sources, you could remove a short part of the road, colliding with the building area, by using the scissor or cutting tool. Just cut the way by pointing the nodes next to the building. So you’ll end up with 2 ways to the building. But be sure that the road has not become a covered way or tunnel ! If so you’ll still have to cut the way into 3 different parts to tag it correctly.
