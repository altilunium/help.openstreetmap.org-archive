I wouldn't add something based just on a website, for a couple of reasons. One is that the website might be a "ghost" of a business that folded long ago (I can think of plenty of local pub websites like that); another is that for some business types (locksmiths, rubbish removal) often the website info is nearly or actually fraudulent. The wiki has an article "verifiability".
