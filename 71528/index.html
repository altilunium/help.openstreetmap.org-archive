OSM nor OSMF provide such services. You have to contact one of the many companies listed on the wiki

The services offered by OSM/OSMF are free, but with restrictions. Those restrictions are typically OK for hobbyist and mappers, but not for commercial purposes. You cannot buy a license to lift those restrictions.
