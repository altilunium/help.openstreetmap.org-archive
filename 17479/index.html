The traffic signals wiki page rather clearly states that there is no well established convention currently. You can either add one single traffic signal at the crosspoint of the roads (example here) or one traffic signal of each incoming way of a crossing (examples here and here). It also depends on whether the sidewalks and bicycle ways are mapped separately or not.

Also don't forget about the crossing tag where you can specify various kinds of crossings.
