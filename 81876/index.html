I'm not a Garmin user myself these days, but this page indicates that there has been general success with your model.

Premade maps of regions are available from the sources listed here. Note that the garmin.openstreetmap.nl site has been unreliable enough recently to generate quite a few questions on this help site, so you may wish to try another provider first.

I have not tried their Garmin maps, but I have found extract.bbbike.org's other offerings quite useful for custom areas. They offer several Garmin map styles.

If you would like to generate your own files or need instructions on how to transfer them to your device I think this page is probably the place to start.
