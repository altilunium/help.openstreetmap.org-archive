I'm not to fond of the start_date tag, but I usually tag the construction years, architect and start_date anyways.

This would be a building for me:

  • building=residential
  • construction_date=2008-2011
  • start_date=2011
  • architect=Åwl; Frans Bengtsson

And the landuse=construction would have been tagged with:

  • opening_date=Autumn 2010
  • start_date=2008
  • architect=Åwl; Fran Bengtsson
  • website=http://www.awlark.se/

The architect tag is a bit free form, but so is the date tags unfortunately. I also like to add websites to construction areas, which tend to be a bit free form as well.
