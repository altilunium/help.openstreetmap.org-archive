The resolution of the image download facility is limited, and hence whether or not you can get it to display street names depends on the size of the area you are downloading.

Use the search box above to search for "render my own map" to find out how you can generate a higher-resolution map on your own computer, or try one of these services/tools:

  • https://maposmatic.osm-baustelle.de/
  • http://printmaps-osm.de:8080/
  • http://bigmap.osmz.ru/
