This is due to a change in the OSM OAuth verification.

It has been fixed in Maps 3.38.6, 40.3 (and in the 41.0 released after this was applied): https://gitlab.gnome.org/GNOME/gnome-maps/-/issues/386

This change has also affected JOSM: https://josm.openstreetmap.de/ticket/21065
