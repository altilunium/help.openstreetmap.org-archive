Do you mean OpenTripPlanner (OTP)? A link to the OTP instance which you are using would be useful.

Two possible reasons:

  • You've inserted the footway two days ago - that may be too recent (find out which data is used for routing by the OTP instance you are using). Usually routing engines significantly lag behind the main OSM database.
  • Furthermore your footway's western node is not connected to anything (which may be conforming with reality). I don't know what you choose as target in the routing and if the OTP has a "direct connection/straight line" (no way) routing capability. Depending on which position you choose as routing target (e.g. the middle of the runways) it may be too far away (for OTP) from your footway.
