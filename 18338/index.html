Taginfo does a relatively good job of spidering the Wiki and storing tag documentation in a SQLite database. That database can be downloaded in full from http://taginfo.openstreetmap.org/download/taginfo-wiki.db.bz2 - the only thing missing is a viewer for this in JOSM.
