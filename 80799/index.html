I just tried editing in that Missing Maps task of yours as well. In that task you are supposed to map buildings and residential areas. I guess that is the reason why you cannot find roads in the list of objects. If you map an area you can select between building, residential area, village, hamlet and isolated dwelling.

So I think the simple answer is: roads have been deactivated for this task to focus you on what the task is about.
