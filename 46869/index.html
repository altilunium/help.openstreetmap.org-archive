I think [1] is still impossible. But you can do [2]! Click on the "Share" button (left). In the column that appears on the right, the lowest part is about downloading. Download as "Full map data". This gives you a .umap file. Create a new umap, and before doing anything else, click "upload data" and choose your umap file.
