We can't help you directly with third party apps as we have no control over how they work and how and when they generate their map data.

What you can do is to use the routing (and search) functionality on openstreetmap.org and see if you can find and route to the road in question. If you report back here, we may be able to determine what is going wrong (if it doesn't actually work).
