Should train station parking be tagged as landuse:railway ?

1

While the parking's primary use is for the station and it is directly attached to it, the wiki page about landuse:railway mentions that the zone is for railways, the station itself and any maintenance equipment or buildings supporting the operation.

Should the parking stay in the landuse:railway area or should it be removed from it, making the zone cover only equipment and buildings related to the operations of the trains, as stated on the wiki ?
