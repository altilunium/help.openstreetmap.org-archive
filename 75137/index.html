Yes, they are very definitely welcome. Really there is no such thing as "too small" in this context - if a path or trail exists in reality, and people really use it for walking and/or cycling, it certainly belongs in OpenStreetMap. In fact one of the things I personally like about OpenStreetMap is that it has better coverage of paths than commercial mapping products, in my area at least.

There are two main ways of mapping paths: https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:highway%3Dpath https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Tag:highway%3Dfootway It is not always obvious which of these to choose, but either way it is often helpful to add other tags such as "surface" if you know these. I would guess that what you refer to as single-tracks fits better with highway=path, but if possible have a look at how other people have mapped similar things in your country.
