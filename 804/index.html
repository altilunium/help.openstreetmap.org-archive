As Andy says, cycling is permitted on trunk roads in the UK unless expressly forbidden, in which case you would use a bicycle=no tag. (There are only four significant examples I can think of where it's forbidden - the Edinburgh Southern Bypass, the A1 just east of Edinburgh, the western section of the A465 Heads of the Valleys Road, and parts of the A55 North Wales Coast Road.)
