The Export dialog is for exporting raw data, not images.

You need the Share dialog - there should be an icon in the list at the right of your screen. This gives you an option to share as an Image, which in turn offers a number of formats incluidng PNG.

https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Export
