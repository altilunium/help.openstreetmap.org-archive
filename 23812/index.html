The Case Studies on switch2osm.org offer a small selection of prominent examples.

In addition to that, the OpenStreetMap wiki does have much more comprehensive lists of almost everything, but it's not always easy to find the right one:

  • OSM Internet Links contains sites using OSM (the German page is a lot longer)
  • They are using OpenStreetMap is a list of organizations using OSM (not limited to websites)
  • List of OSM based Services contains mostly specialist or experimental map styles; you won't find sites that just embed standard maps
  • Software has links to lists and tables of applications for various platforms

A few of the switch2osm.org case studies are related to real estate business and may be relevant to your interests.
