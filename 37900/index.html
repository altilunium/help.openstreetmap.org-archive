I could not find such an option in JOSM (also not in the advanced options - quickly scanning). You may want to report it as bug/feature request for JOSM to the developers (if you want to do so, please search the old bugs/"tickets" before and after you've done please mention the link to the report here).

In the meantime you could disable the feature on your Mac under Preferences-Mouse.
