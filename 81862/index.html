There's a link on the routing page that goes to here. That says:

Overview of routing data updates

The routing data are updated daily. There may be delays in the internal processing and creation of the routing diagram.

The downloadable "foot Europe Asia" timestamp file contains "2021-09-21T15:00:00Z", which is just over a day ago. Once that date is after your update, it'll be using your data.
